Our Ministries

MEN'S FELLOWSHIP

Men’s Fellowship is made up of married men; they are in the position of honor and move in full force to the Glory of God. Men’s ministry aim at mankind -everyman useful to himself, church and society and to ensure total believe in God no matter opposition.

The Men's Ministry also aim at educating the men as touching business and their endeavors. And, of course, they organize seminars, conferences and teachings.

WOMEN'S FELLOWSHIP

Women Fellowship united all sisters both married and singles that worship in Dominion Cathedral (Headquarters). They have been a blessing to the church, sisters and the society in general; they ensure the visible prove of spiritual growth of the sisters through undiluted and inspirational teachings, seminars and conferences.

Special meetings and prayer hours are been organized in order to enrich, educate, strengthen, enlighten the spiritual lives of our sisters.

During prayer meetings we do experience the move of God, presence and “Tsidkenu Glory” of Jehovah. Our Women Fellowship inculcates in the lives of our sisters the need to be committed to the service of God; also to love their husbands equally; to be a faithful mother to their children and in the church to be exemplary mothers. Our Women Fellowship supports the less privileged in the society.

CHILDREN'S FELLOWSHIP

DICM (Dominion Cathedral) Children fellowship is called the Children of Light. Our objectives are to introduce Christ to our children at their early stage; to inculcate our good values and morals in their lives. And in other hand, make them believe in themselves.

We also stir the interest of our children in the knowledge of the word of God. The children department is to ensure that the children are introduced to Scriptures (The Bible) and things pertaining to God and godliness from their childhood through their teenage life as they grow into mature individuals.

The children are open to Child Christian Ministration which can come in diverse ways such as in Bible Memorization, Music, Bible Quiz, and Excursions and so on.

YOUTH'S FELLOWSHIP

DICM (Dominion Cathedral) Youth Fellowship is made of vibrant people in the vineyard of God. They are energetic and full of energy; vigour and are ready to do the God’s work. The objective of the Youth Fellowship is to influence every youth for God and influence the society for Christ; also to inculcate the consciousness of heaven in the lives of youth. In addition to make every youth maximize his potentials, gifts and talents in the vineyard of God.
